Cats | Dogs

Yes, I love Both cats and dogs. If we get to know each other, you'll get to know this because I talk about my animals, like, a lot. I even have them on my mousepad, see that over there? If you want to see more of these sweeties give that mousepad a click.

Lucy was my first pet, and she is a rescue kitty. I rescued her directly, actually. I found her at just a couple of days old when out with some friends one evening. She needed help, and despite being very allergic I couldn't say no. Now she is my number one workspace invader.

Rainer was also a rescue, and has been with the family for about 2 years now. He had terrible seperation anxiety, but with lots of love he is now the most good boy (I tell him every day). He never barks, and is always the most popular guy at the Dog Park

I'm more than just cats and dogs though, click that arrow to see more.

Music

Like so many, I too am a lover of music. I love to play as well as consume. Music is almost always a great place for me to start a conversation when getting to know someone new.

Some favorite genres include: post rock, folk/grassroots, hip hop, indie rock, psych rock, bluegrass--the list really goes on. I had a radio show in college playing post rock, so I know the most there.

I wanted to share a few clips of songs I like with you, so click that record icon to see my record player and pick a selection. When you're finished, click that arrow.

Books

Since I used to teach English and have my MA in Literature, it goes without saying that I love to read. I enjoy a range of books, but my favorites these days are literary Modernism and Postmodernism (I also do like Harry Potter).

Poetry was my first love in reading, and it remains something I love today. There are certain poems that were at one time "my favorite" that I always keep on my bookshelf still. Click the book icon over there to see the shelf I'm keeping them on and read a few if you'd like.
